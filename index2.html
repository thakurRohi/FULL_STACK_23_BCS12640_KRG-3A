Day 7 (Mon, Aug 4): Consolidation & Contest Simulation

Today is about applying your knowledge under time pressure. The most important part is the deep analysis and up-solving session afterward.

  1. Participate in a Virtual Contest (2 hours): Pick a recent Codeforces Division 2 contest. Treat it like a real exam.
  2. Deep Upsolving Session (4+ hours):
    • Attempt to solve every problem you couldn't finish, starting from the easiest.
    • Spend at least 1 hour trying to solve a problem on your own before looking at the editorial.
    • Read the editorial, understand the core idea, and then try to code it from scratch without copy-pasting.
    • Analyze why you couldn't solve it during the contest. Was it a knowledge gap, a bug, or time pressure?
